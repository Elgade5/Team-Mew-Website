Team Rules

  1. Respect all players & staff.
  2. Stay active & communicate.
  3. No cheating or exploits.
  4. Keep a positive reputation.
  5. Have fun & play hard.
  6. Keep it clean-ish. Light swearing is fine. NSFW content is not.
  7. Stay on topic. Use the correct channels for your posts.
  8. Shiny Charm Etiquette
    • Requests: Be polite, no begging. One ask is enough.
    • Invites: Never invite others without the host's permission.

Breaking rules → warning → removal.
